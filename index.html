We are building a tightly integrated wearable to keep distractions away.

With the Ringify One, wearers can simply tap, press, or swipe on the ring instead of pulling out their smartphone for repeated simple tasks, or more serious ones that can take advantage of it's minimalist, one handed form factor. And with intelligent notification filtering, users can know to finally pull out their phone when something important comes up.

Tech Specs

Each Ringify One is equipped with an ARM-based processor that talks over BLE to your smartphone. With our app installed, a user can map intuitive ring gestures to smartphone actions such as music controls, notification dismissal, you name it!

  • 64MHz ARM Processor

  • Optical Tap/Swipe Sensing

  • Integrated 6-Axis IMU

  • SpO2 and HR Sensor

All of this in a discreet package with intuitive controls.
