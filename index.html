We are building a tightly integrated wearable to keep distractions away.

Users can simply tap, press, or swipe on the ring instead of pulling out their smartphone for repeated tasks. Take advantage of intelligent notification delivery and the ring's one-handed interface to get things done without the hassle.

Tech Specs

Each smart ring is equipped with an ARM-based processor that talks over BLE to your smartphone. With our app installed, a user can map intuitive ring gestures to smartphone actions such as music controls, notification dismissal, you name it!

  • 64MHz ARM Processor

  • Tap/Swipe Sensing

  • Integrated 6-Axis IMU

  • SpO2 and HR Sensor

All of this in a discreet package with intuitive controls.
